SWEETS RECIPE

Barfi

Ingredients

  • sugar, Salt, Nuts
  • Ghee, Semolina
  • Milk, Milk powder
  • Cardamom

Recipe

  1. Heat ghee on medium heat then add milk & continue to heat the mixture till it is at room temp.
  2. Start adding milk powder. Use a whisk to smooth out granular pieces.
  3. Add nuts and almonds, continue stirring the mixture.
  4. Take the mixture put it on a plate and place it in the fridge.

Champakali

Ingredients

  • sugar, food coloring
  • Ghee or oil
  • All purpose flour

Recipe

  1. Add ajwain, red chili powder, cumin seeds, salt to the flour mix.
  2. Mix it well and now add a little amount of water and knead the dough.
  3. Once soft dough is ready stat making traditional champakali.

Gulab jamun

Ingredients

  • Milk, Milk powder
  • flour, baking powder
  • Sugar, water, Ghee

Recipe

  1. Stir milk & baking powder,flour then mix in melted ghee, pour milk & continue to mix.
  2. Stir together sugar,water and a pinch of cardamom.
  3. Knead the dough & make small balls then fry them.
  4. Place the balls in the syrup.
  5. Ready to serve
